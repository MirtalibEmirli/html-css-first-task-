X-make life's best

X.com is currently a social media platform that was formerly known as Twitter. Elon Musk, the owner of Twitter, rebranded the platform as "X" in 2023 as part of his broader vision of transforming it into an "everything app" similar to WeChat in China. Musk's goal for X.com is to go beyond social media, incorporating services like payments, shopping, and other utilities, making it a multi-functional platform. Before this, X.com was originally an online payment company founded by Elon Musk in 1999. It later merged with another company and eventually became PayPal, which is now a leading online payments service.
